## Persona 1: Priya — The Hustling Freelancer

| Attribute       | Detail                                      |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Age**         | 26                                          |
| **Occupation**  | Freelance Graphic Designer                  |
| **Location**    | Mumbai, India                               |
| **Tech Savvy**  | Moderate                                    |
| **Device**      | Laptop (primary), Android phone (secondary) |

### Background
Priya juggles 4–6 clients at any time, working from cafes and co-working spaces. She left her agency job a year ago to go independent. She handles her own invoicing, client communication, and finances — there's no admin team behind her.

### Goals
- Send professional-looking invoices quickly after completing a project
- Avoid monthly subscriptions for tools she only uses a few times a month
- Share invoices with clients via WhatsApp or email without attachments getting lost

### Pain Points
- Most invoicing tools require sign-up and lock features behind paywalls
- Free tools slap watermarks on exported PDFs, making her look unprofessional
- She doesn't want to store sensitive client data on yet another third-party platform

### How QuillBill Helps
- No sign-up means she can go from zero to invoice in under a minute
- Clean, professional templates make her brand look established
- Shareable links let her send invoices via any messaging app — no PDF attachment hassle
- No server-side storage means her clients' data stays private

### Quote
> *"I just want to send an invoice, not create another account I'll forget the password to."*
